# Browser Bridge — Name Ideas

NOTE FROM ERIC: Gargoyle is my runner up, the image of high perch matches the browser and its goofy

> Shortlist of names for the `@promptuary/browser-bridge` package. Goal: memorable, slightly unexpected, hints at "an agent looking into a browser."

## Top Tier

| Name | Why it works | Risk |
|------|-------------|------|
| **Fovea** | The center of the retina where vision is sharpest. "The agent's sharpest view into your app." Short, biological, zero tech collisions. | Sounds medical to some |
| **Aperture** | An opening. Photographic, precise, clean. "The aperture between your agent and your browser." | Slightly generic |
| **Séance** | You summon an agent into your browser session. Spiritual, spooky, unforgettable. "Start a séance with your app." | May sound occult to enterprise buyers |
| **Familiar** | A witch's companion spirit that sees and reports back. "Your agent is your familiar." | Too D&D? |
| **Oculus** | Latin for eye. Circular opening. Classic. | Taken by Meta |

## Strong Contenders

| Name | Why it works |
|------|-------------|
| **Periscope** | The agent peeks into your browser from its submarine. Playful. |
| **Monocle** | Sophisticated single eye. "The agent's monocle on your page." |
| **Prism** | Refracts browser state into agent understanding. |
| **Haunt** | The agent haunts your browser tab. Short, punchy, slightly dark. |
| **Conduit** | Direct pipe between agent and browser. Boring but descriptive. |
| **Tether** | The agent is tethered to your browser session. |
| **Pupil** | The opening in the eye. Simple, eye-related, very short. |
| **Side channel** | Actual security term for extracting info through an unintended path. Very apt technically. | Too jargon-y? |

## Wild / Memorable

| Name | Vibe |
|------|------|
| **Poltergeist** | The agent rattles around in your browser |
| **Imago** | Final form. The agent's imago sees through the browser |
| **Tapetum** | Reflective layer behind the retina that helps night vision. "See in the dark." |
| **Symbiote** | Agent and browser living in symbiosis |
| **Spectacle** | Both "specter" (ghost) and "spectacle" (sight) |
| **Gargoyle** | Grotesque watcher perched on your browser |

## My Recommendation

**Fovea** if you want sophisticated and unique. **Séance** if you want unforgettable and slightly playful. **Aperture** if you want safe and clean.
